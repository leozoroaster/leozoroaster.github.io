About me

I am a first-year PhD student in the CS Department at Princeton University, advised by Prof. Elad Hazan. Before Princeton, I received B.S. in Statistics from Peking University in 2018, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Liwei Wang.

Research

My research interests lie in machine learning theory, especially expressivity of neural networks and online learning. I am a fan of constructive proofs in proving bounds.
